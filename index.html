Babette Hofacre

M.A. CCC-SLP

Babette Hofacre (she/they) is a licensed speech-language pathologist who has a specialized interest in voice. She received her M.A. in speech-language pathology from George Washington University and is currently licensed in Utah, DC, and Virginia. Babette completed the Summer Vocology Institute (SVI) program in Salt Lake City, Utah where she gained in-depth knowledge on voice disorders, gender-affirming therapy, and singing/acting voice.

Babette was raised in Santa Cruz, California and currently lives in Salt Lake City, Utah. She passes her time playing the banjo and walking in the mountains.
